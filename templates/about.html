About GDDES

Welcome to GDDES (Gynecological Disease Diagnosis Expert System) — a visionary initiative that combines the power of machine learning and modern healthcare to assist in the early detection of gynecological disorders, with a primary focus on PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) and UTI (Urinary Tract Infection).

GDDES leverages state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms to analyze user input in the form of medical parameters and symptoms, then generates intelligent predictions about the user’s potential condition. It’s more than just a tool — it’s an interactive expert system that empowers individuals to take control of their health

We continue to enhance GDDES with better models, smarter diagnostics, and broader disease coverage — because we believe that every woman deserves access to quality health insights, right at her fingertips.
